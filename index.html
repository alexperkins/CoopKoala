WHAT WE DO

The goal of CoopKoala is to shed some light on the unknown when it comes to getting your first job. There are many students who have walked in the shoes of your potential co-op, and this site will give them the opportunity to share what it’s truly like to work there. Other sites may feature a standard review system, but we are bringing our own “Day in the Life” timeline to give a better representation of what it’s really like to work in the field. Our service will better guarantee you that you will be receiving a Koality-Coop!
